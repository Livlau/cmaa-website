Welcoming Angel Ceremony - Khmer New Year 2023

Cambodians celebrate their new year annually in the middle of April. Traditionally, Cambodians spend 3 days enjoying the Khmer New Year. This year, the celebration starts from the 14th to the 16th of April 2023. According to Khmer traditions based on an old Hindu astrology, Cambodians believe that on Friday, April 14, 2023 at 4:00PM (Cambodian Time) or Friday, April 13, 2022 at 5:00AM (Eastern Time USA), the Sun passes from Mina Rashi (Pisces) to Mesa Rashi (Aries), marking a complete revolution thus starting a new cycle.

Cambodians believe that the new year angels descend from the heavens of the Four Great Kings to bless them. The leading angel of this year is called Kemira Devi; the sixth daughter of Kbal Maha Prum, holding a Sword on her left hand, a Harp on her right hand, and riding a water buffalo.

The first day, April 14th, is called Maha Sangkran (the great transmigration of the Sun). Second day, April 15th is called Virak Vanabat (the blind day). The third day, April 16th, is called Virak Lerng Sak "the new year day". During the three days, Cambodians are praying for happiness, friendship, kindness, harmony, and forgiveness toward others.
